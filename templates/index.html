My Mission

To help as many people as I can to reach the goal of collecting 1,000 cranes.

Overview

Welcome to the Cranes of Hope page. According to an ancient Japanese story, anyone who is presented with a thousand origami cranes will be granted a wish such as long life or recovery from illness or injury. I want to fill the people with HOPE. To do this I plan to spend hours making cranes for people with cancer.

General Information

I put at least 2 hearts on every crane for my motto; "From my heart to yours". I also put uplifting quotes on many of my cranse as well:

  • Live Strong
  • Be Strong
  • U R Loved
  • Live with hope in your heard
  • Be Brave
  • Courage
  • Strength
  • Love Hope Faith

I fill and refill baskets with my cranes and bring them to oncology offices. I also teach classes on how to make cranes and hope to inspire others to do good.
